Our Story

A Tradition of Excellence

Founded on the principles of community and athletic excellence, GNG Cricket Club has evolved from a small collective of enthusiasts into a powerhouse of Kent cricket.

Our journey is marked by memorable matches at the Gravesend grounds, where generations of players have honed their skills under the iconic burgundy and gold banner. We've weathered seasons of transition, always emerging stronger and more unified.

"Today, our trophy cabinet reflects our competitive drive, but our real achievement lies in the lasting bonds formed on the pitch."
