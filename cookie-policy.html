Cookie Policy

This page explains how GNG Cricket Club uses cookies and similar browser storage technologies on this website.

Last updated: 8 April 2026

How Cookies Are Used

Cookies and similar technologies help websites remember settings, measure visits, and understand how visitors use pages. On this website, non-essential analytics cookies are only loaded if you choose to accept them.

Cookies and Storage Used on This Site

Cookie preference storage

We store your cookie choice in your browser so the site can remember whether you accepted or rejected non-essential cookies. This is used to manage your privacy preference.

Google Analytics cookies

If you accept analytics cookies, Google Analytics may set cookies to help the club understand site traffic, page visits, and general usage patterns. These cookies are not loaded until you actively opt in.

Managing Your Choice

You can accept or reject non-essential cookies using the banner shown when you first visit the site. You can reopen those controls at any time using the Cookie Settings link in the footer.

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings. If you delete or block cookies in your browser, some stored preferences may reset.

Third-Party Cookies

If analytics cookies are accepted, Google may process related usage data under its own terms and privacy documentation. This website also links to external services such as Facebook and Instagram. Those services may set cookies when you visit them, but they are outside this website's control.
